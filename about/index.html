What I'm Doing

I am slowly making a website containing different pages that hold information from different sources of fiction that are about creatures found in those wonderful worlds of imagination.

Eventual Pages

Dragons
I will have a page specifically for the different types of Dragons and give information that is specific to those types of dragons.
Giants
There are multiple types of giants depending on what sources you get them from. For example D&D has Storm Giants and Fire Giants while Harry Potter simply has Giants and Half-Giants.
Other Misc Creatures
To name a few I am interested in eventually adding there are Basilisks, The Nemean Lion, Direwolves, Goblins, Werewolves, and Elementals.

Sources of Material

Dungeons and Dragons is a role-playing game that contains many different types of monsters and has books with information about them.

Harry Potter is a book series written by J.K. Rowling that contains some information on magical creatures.
